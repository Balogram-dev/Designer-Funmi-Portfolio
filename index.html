My Design Process

The Oluwaseunfunmi Method

1. Understand

I dive deep to uncover the "why" behind the project. Through stakeholder interviews, user research, and competitive analysis, I get to know the people I'm designing for — their needs, behaviors, and pain points.

2. Define

I make sense of the chaos. I craft user personas, define clear problem statements, and align on goals. This stage turns research into a roadmap. If we’re solving the wrong problem, it doesn’t matter how , beautiful the solution is..

3. Ideate

Time to explore! I brainstorm solutions, sketch ideas, and map out user flows. This is where imagination meets intention. No idea is too wild,, every great product starts with a spark..

4. Wireframe

With direction in place, I move to low-fidelity wireframes. This helps structure the experience and plan out the journey, fast,, focused, and flexible. Clarity first, color later..

5. Design

Here comes the visual magic. I buildhigh-fidelity designs using a consistent design system, creating interfaces that are both functional and beautiful. Design should, delight, not distract..

6. Prototype

I bring ideas to life with clickable prototypes. This helps clients and users feel the product, before it's built, making feedback richer and faster. Test the experience before writing a single line of code..

7. Test & Iterate

I observe real users interacting with the design, gather insights, and refine. , Feedback isn’t just welcomed, it’s essential..

What I Do (And Do Really Well)

Product Design (UI/UX)

I design intuitive, user-centered digital products—from mobile apps to complex dashboards. I focus on functionality, accessibility, and aesthetic balance to create interfaces users love.

UX Research & Strategy

Design that's backed by data, not just vibes. I don't guess—I ask, test, observe, and learn. I dig deep into user behaviors and needs to craft informed design strategies that solve real problems.

UX Writing

Words matter. I craft clear, consistent, and meaningful microcopy that guides users and enhances experience — from buttons and tooltips to onboarding flows and error messages. Every word earns its place.

Design Mentorship & Tutoring

I help aspiring designers level up through practical, hands-on learning. Whether it's 1-on-1 sessions, bootcamps, or workshops—I make design less scary and more exciting. From newbie to ninja, I've got you covered.

Design Systems

I build scalable, consistent design systems that keep your brand unified and your team efficient. Components, tokens, rules—you name it. Because chaos doesn't scale. Systems do.

Have an idea, a product, or a team that needs guidance? I offer design direction, feedback, and collaborative sessions to help you move forward with clarity. Sometimes, all you need is a fresh perspective.
